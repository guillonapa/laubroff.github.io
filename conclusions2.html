Conclusions Part Two

Though no correlation could be found between “green” counties and their voting patterns, I think that the information I found and analyzed is still important. For many people, the presidential election of 2016 was a big surprise, and a rude awakening. If a democratic president is to win the next election, and/or we are able to implement more environmental action in our country, it will take more planning and analyzing than usual. These three states are examples of where our attention can be focused, in order to both boost democratic voting as well as environmental action and support.

I experienced a lot of difficulties in finding data for this half of the project, ultimately I was able to find it, but it took much more time and effort. The map making in GIS was also more difficult, mostly due to small problems with the data that needed to be fixed before mapping. Overall, working on this project as a whole really helped me to understand the skills I was learning in class, and have a better understanding of ArcGIS as a whole.
