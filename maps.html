Maps

When viewed all together, it is easy to see slight correlations between states that are high on the green scale, had a higher voter turnout, and voted for Hillary. Regardless of who people voted for, a connection can be seen between states that are environmentally conscious, and states whose voters understand the importance of voting. When voting results are added to this equation, it is then clear that in addition to understanding the importance of casting a vote, these states were generally voting for Hillary Clinton.
